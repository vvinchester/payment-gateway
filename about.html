About Valkyrie's Valor

Valkyrie's Valor Foundation collaborates with elementary school teachers and their schools to create vibrant and engaging classrooms focused on literacy and learning. By providing colorful books, teacher training, and support for school programs, we strive to develop core literacy skills and foster a lifelong love of reading. Our goal is to empower students, teachers, and schools to create a nurturing environment where education flourishes and children thrive.

Together, let's join hands for Valkyrie's Valor Foundation, creating vibrant classrooms and fostering a love for literacy and learning, empowering young minds to reach their full potential.

The Focus of Our Work

Literacy & Education

Education programs, driven by the belief that curious kids can change the world. Valkyrie's Valor has spent decades improving education quality in marginalized indigenous communities, acknowledging the strong link between illiteracy and systemic poverty. Our commitment is to create a lasting impact on children's lives through education.

Primary Schools

We prioritize primary schools as the cornerstone of our efforts because early literacy holds paramount importance. Valkyrie's Valor recognizes the transformative power of developing foundational literacy skills in children during the early grades. By fostering self-sufficiency and adaptability, we empower children to navigate and thrive in a changing world.

Uttarpradesh and Bihar

It is a place of great need and great opportunity. UP and Bihar are regions marked by significant challenges such as high rates of illiteracy, inequality, and poverty. Through collaborative efforts, we work with the people of these regions, striving to achieve a shared vision of a more prosperous and equitable society where peace and prosperity are accessible to all.

Coaching

Coaching is the secret of our success. Understanding the significance of direct teacher engagement within classrooms, we recognize that it is essential for cultivating improved educators. This approach fosters collaborative trust, instills confidence to embrace innovation, and unites us in our shared mission of facilitating children's learning.
